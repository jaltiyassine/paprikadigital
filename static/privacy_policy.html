PRIVACY POLICY

At Paprikadigital, accessible from https://paprikadigital.net/, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document outlines the information we collect from users who fill out the "Request a Quote" form and how we use that information.

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.

This Privacy Policy applies only to our online activities and is valid for visitors to our website with regards to the information that they share and/or collect in Paprikadigital. This policy is not applicable to any information collected offline or via channels other than this website.

1. Information We Collect

When you fill out the "Request Your Custom Solution" form on our website, we collect the following information:

  • Company Name: To identify your business or organization.
  • Full Name: To communicate with you personally.
  • Email Address: To send you updates, quotes, or relevant information regarding your request.
  • Phone Number: (Optional) To contact you for more immediate communication regarding your request.
  • Type of Solution: To understand the type of digital service you need (e.g., website, mobile app, desktop app).
  • Project Description: To gather details about your project and requirements.
  • Budget Range: To determine if your project aligns with your financial expectations.
  • Timeline: To understand your deadlines and urgency.
  • Preferred Platform/Technology: (Optional) To know your technology preferences, if any.
  • Files: (Optional) To upload relevant documents that help us understand your project better.
  • Additional Comments: (Optional) To include any other information you wish to provide for a more accurate quote or proposal.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • Project Understanding: To assess your requirements and provide an accurate quote or proposal for the requested digital service (e.g., website, mobile app, desktop app).
  • Communication: To contact you via email or phone to discuss your project further, answer questions, or provide updates.
  • Service Improvement: To enhance our offerings, evaluate user needs, and improve the overall user experience on our website.
  • Legal and Compliance: To comply with any legal requirements or obligations.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with trusted partners or service providers who help us operate our website, process transactions, or provide services (e.g., payment processors). We may also disclose your information to comply with legal obligations or protect our rights.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to process your request, provide services, or comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion or modification of your personal information by contacting us directly.

6. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information.
  • Request the deletion of your personal data.
  • Opt out of receiving promotional emails or communication.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We recommend that you review this page periodically for any changes. Changes to the Privacy Policy are effective immediately once posted on this page.

8. Consent

By filling out the "Request Your Custom Solution" form, you consent to the collection, use, and storage of your personal and project-related information as outlined in this Privacy Policy.
